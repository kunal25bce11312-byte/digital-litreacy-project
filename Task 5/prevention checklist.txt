✔️ Stay Safe Online – Cyber Safety Checklist

Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone.
Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links.
Always check the URL before entering banking details.
Use official apps like bank apps or trusted UPI apps only.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security.
Do not accept unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps.
Keep your phone and apps updated regularly.
Install antivirus/security apps on your device.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
Immediately block your account if suspicious activity is noticed.
